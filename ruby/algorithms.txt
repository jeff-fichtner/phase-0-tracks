#Robot Elevator Algorithm

- Approach elevator
- Until elevator doors are open
	If elevator is not called
		- call elevator
		- wait
	Else
		- wait
- Enter elevator
- Decide the floor you want to arrive at
- Find button corresponding to that floor
- Press button
- Wait until elevator doors close
- Wait until elevator moves in the direction of the chosen floor
If elevator doors open on chosen floor
	- Exit the elevator
Else
	- Wait

End.
